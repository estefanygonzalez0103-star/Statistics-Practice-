Step 1 — What is the MEAN? (sum ÷ 8)
8+10+7+10+9+6+10+8 = 68. Then 68 ÷ 8 = ?
Step 2 — What is the MEDIAN? (Sorted: 6, 7, 8, 8, 9, 10, 10, 10 → average the 4th and 5th values)

Final Challenge — Full Analysis
🌡️ A scientist records daily high temperatures (°C) for 2 weeks: 24, 27, 22, 29, 31, 25, 28, 26, 30, 23, 27, 25, 29, 27.
Step 1 — What is the MODE temperature?
Count each: 27 appears 3 times (days 2, 11, 14). No other temperature appears that many times.
Step 2 — The sum of all 14 temperatures is 372°C. What is the MEAN?
372 ÷ 14 = 26.57... ≈ 26.6°C
Step 3 — Sorted: 22,23,24,25,25,26,27,27,27,28,29,29,30,31. Find the MEDIAN. (14 values → average the 7th and 8th)
7th value = 27, 8th value = 27. Median = (27+27)÷2 = 27.
